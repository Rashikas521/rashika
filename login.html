Nokia is a Finnish multinational corporation that provides telecommunications, information technology, and consumer electronics. The company was founded in 1865 as a paper mill in Tampere, Finland.

Vision: Nokia wants to create a new world; to transform a big planet to a small village. Their vision is to create, build, and encourage people from all countries to communicate with each other in order to create a world where everybody is connected.

Mission: Nokia envisions a world where everyone is connected, bridging the digital divide to ensure accessibility for all. They believe technology should go beyond basic communication, enabling rich experiences that foster shared connections and enrich lives. This includes advancements in virtual and augmented reality to facilitate seamless interactions across geographical boundaries.
